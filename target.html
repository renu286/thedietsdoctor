Lifestyle Management Plans

PCOD/PCOS

PCOD/PCOS (polycystic ovarian syndrome/disorder) is an endocrine system disorder that affectsa woman’s hormone level. Women with PCOD/PCOS produces more male hormones by the ovary due to insulin resistance, genetic, pollution and inflammation, that leads to several health issues (weight gain, irregular periods, hair growth, darkness of skin, etc.).
